About Us



D-Money Team Records is a recording and studio company based in Kempton Park with over five years in the industry,our techinicians are
well experienced in sound recording, video and editing to proffesional levels. We have several movies, weddings and funerals to our
name which have been proffesionally done. We record weddings, functions thus birthday parties, graduation, church services and
funerals across the country and anywhere withing Southern African where the client desires. We are based in the central Kempton Park
close to the airport so we are within the reach of many in South Africa.


We are available for any wedding functions for recording and picture shooting any day of the week. We are at your service.
